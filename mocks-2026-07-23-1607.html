Completely rebuilt since the previous mocks, which showed a +1 / +5 / +10 preset counter. Both steppers seed from banked totals rather than 0, and only the delta is written to logs. The − button floors at the banked total — there is no un-logging. With makes on there is no upper clamp: a real 30-attempt session against a 25 target has to survive intact, because attempts are the denominator of the percentage.
